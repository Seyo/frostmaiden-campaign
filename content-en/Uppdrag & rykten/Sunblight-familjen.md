[[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s last words: *"My father rides on a dragon's back and shall destroy all of Ten-Towns."*

- The Sunblight family claims all of Icewind Dale belongs to them.
- Nildar's father is alive and represents a serious coming threat.
- The connection to a dragon is unknown but ominous.
- Nildar ran a fortress at [[Kelvins Cairn]] with grey dwarves, cells for fungus-infected prisoners, and an alchemy laboratory.
