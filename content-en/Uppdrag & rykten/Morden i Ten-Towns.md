- **Victims (ice-pick murders):** Halfling hunter (Easthaven), human shipbuilder (Targos), possibly a dwarf (Caer Konig — unconfirmed, see below).
- **Modus Operandi:** Ice pick through the heart.
- **Theory:** Connected to [[Auril]] or a cult.
- **Status:** Unsolved. The Easthaven and Targos murders remain uninvestigated.

> **Note:** The murder in [[Caer Konig]] turned out to be [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] (throat slit, not an ice pick) — a separate crime carried out by [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s dwarves. It is unclear whether "the dwarf in Caer Konig" is a different, separate victim of the ice-pick killer.
