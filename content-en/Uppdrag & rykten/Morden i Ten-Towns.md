**Connection Revealed (Day 34):** All victims bear **a snowflake carved/frost-burned into their left shoulder**. The murders coincided with deliveries to the towns from **[[Throgga Icevein(†)|Throgga's Import & Goods]]**. [[Throgga Icevein(†)]] was revealed to be undead — controlled by an unknown power. His employees are the likely killers.

**Confirmed Victims:**
- Halfling hunter (**Easthaven**) — uninvestigated
- Human shipbuilder (**Targos**) — uninvestigated
- **Bromm Runehand** (**Termalaine**, at the harbour, ~Day 25)
- **Fjordis Jewelhand** (**Termalaine**, mine worker, ~Day 30) — stab/slash wounds (3) + snowflake frost-burned into the shoulder

**Modus Operandi:** Stab and slash wounds; snowflake carved/frost-burned into the left shoulder; cold damage around the wounds.

**Suspects (Throgga's employees, hired in Bryn Shander):**
- Magnar (race unknown)
- Fanny Chestnut (race unknown)
- Sirus (red tiefling)
- A **bald human** (bodyguard — fled during the confrontation in Termalaine, Day 34, and vanished without a trace)

**Status:** Ongoing. The bodyguard is missing. The whereabouts of the remaining suspects are unknown.

> **Note:** The murder in [[Caer Konig]] was [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] (throat slit, no snowflake) — a separate crime by [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s dwarves.
