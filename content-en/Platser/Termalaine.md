---
title: "Termalaine"
---

- **Type:** Mining town along the shores of [[Maer Dualdon]], Ten-Towns.
- **Population:** Approx. 600.
- **Character:** Sacrifices warmth to [[Auril]] — freezes willingly. Mines crystals and diamonds.

**Key Locations:**
- **The East Side Inn:** Interconnected buildings via underground tunnels. Innkeeper [[Martha]], close friend of [[Keegan Valryn]] and [[Garret]]. No open fires allowed in the rooms.
- **The Blue Clam:** Tavern. Speaker Oarus Matthews was last seen here — got drunk and disappeared.
- **The Graveyard:** Maintained by **Crank** (gnome). Murder victims are kept here.
- **New Mine:** Closed for ~2 weeks — monsters came up from underground. Foreman Fronda Harroway (dwarf) and all staff are still inside.
- **Old Mine:** Nearly depleted.
- **Fieldspar:** Dwarf jewellery shop, owned by Gillian Fieldspar (from Dwarven Valley). Closed since ~Day 26.

**Key People:**
- **Speaker Oarus Matthews** — town leader, missing since ~Day 33. Secretary **Elka Verryt** may know his whereabouts.
- **Heskam Wyrmhide** — silver-scaled dragonborn, lives in the mine workers' collective.
- **Arno** — mute boy, orphaned (Tribe of the Wolf), adopted by mine foreman Fronda Harroway. Somewhere in town.

**The Murders:**
- **Bromm Runehand** — found at the harbour (~Day 25), first victim in Termalaine.
- **Fjordis Jewelhand** — mine worker (~Day 30). Stab and slash wounds + snowflake frost-burned into the left shoulder.
- The murders coincided with [[Throgga Icevein(†)|Throgga's Import & Goods]] deliveries here. See [[Morden i Ten-Towns]].
