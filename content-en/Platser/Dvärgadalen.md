---
title: "Dwarven Valley"
---

- **Type:** Isolated dwarf community, ~8–10 hours by dog sled from [[Caer Konig]].
- **Character:** The dwarves are highly isolated and independent, distrustful of strangers. Trade in gemstones.
- **Contact:** [[Jarthra]] (guide, *Frozen-fur Expeditions*) helped [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] trade with the dwarves regularly.
- [[Glenn]] at *The Hook Line and Sinker* in Caer Konig imports ale from here.
