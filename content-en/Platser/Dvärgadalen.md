---
title: "Dwarven Valley"
---

- **Type:** Dwarf settlement carved into a ravine, half-cut into the bedrock. A strikingly grand city with tall stone buildings and extraordinary architectural detail. ~8–10 hours by dog sled from [[Caer Konig]].
- **Character:** The dwarves are isolated and independent. Currency is **gemstones** — no coins.
- **The Trading Post:** A suburb just outside the city gate. [[Zahirs gäng]] are now welcome to stay and trade here since Day 24 — but not inside the city itself yet.
- **Known dwarves:** Hruna, Korux, and Storn — were transporting iron ore toward Brynn Shander, rescued by the gang (Day 24). Rewarded the gang with gemstones worth 300 gp.
- **The inn (Day 25):** [[Nasher]] asked the innkeeper about **Dzaan** — she claimed not to know who that is, but was obviously lying.
- **Contact:** [[Jarthra]] (guide) helped [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] trade with the dwarves regularly.
- [[Glenn]] at *The Hook Line and Sinker* in Caer Konig was buying ale from the Sunblight dwarves — not from here. [[Jarthra]] stopped delivering ale to Glenn about 2 years ago.
