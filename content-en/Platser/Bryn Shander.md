---
title: "Bryn Shander"
---

- **Type:** Largest city in Ten-Towns, crowded with refugees.
- **Key locations:**
    - *The Hooked Knucklehead:* Inn where the party rested for a week.
    - *House of the Triad:* Temple (recommended for Nasher).
    - *Reindil's Emporium:* High-end gear, run by a half-elf.
    - *Council Hall:* Home of the sheriff.
- All of [[Zahirs gäng]] are on the sacrifice list.
