# Caer Dineval

[[Platser/Icewind Dale|Icewind Dale]]

Caer Dineval sits on the shore of **Lac Dinneshere**. It is one of the smaller "caer" settlements in the region.

## Campaign Visit

During the party's dog sled journey they passed through Caer Dineval. During the visit:
- The party was forced to pay a fine.
- The party bought **shovels** for future needs.

## Information

The settlement is known for its lakeside location, now gripped by the eternal winter's reach.
