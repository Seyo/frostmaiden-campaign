# Icewind Dale

![[icewind-dale.webp]]

A remote and icebound region at the far north, south of the Sea of Moving Ice and north of the Spine of the World. Home to [[Ten-Towns]] and a range of nomadic barbarian tribes.

## Geography

- **[[Ten-Towns]]** — The ten settlements at the heart of Icewind Dale.
- **[[Kelvins Cairn]]** — Enormous mountain north of [[Caer Konig]]. [[Dvärgadalen]] lies in its shadow.
- **Lac Dinneshere** — The lake by [[Caer Konig]] and [[Caer Dineval]], not frozen despite the extreme cold.
- **Maer Dualdon** — Lake to the west near Bremen, Termalaine, and Lonelywood.
- **Redwaters** — Lake to the south near Good Mead and Dougan's Hole.
- **The Eastway** — Road connecting [[Bryn Shander]], Easthaven, and [[Caer Dineval]].
- **Reghed Glacier** — Vast glacier to the northeast.
- **Spine of the World** — Mountain range marking the southern border of Icewind Dale.
- **Sea of Moving Ice** — Ice-choked sea along the northern coast.

## General

- Perpetual darkness and cold prevail — [[Auril]] has placed a curse over the region that blocks the sun.
- At the border of Icewind Dale a magical darkness falls; light spells do not function.
- The weather is extreme and unpredictable. Hailstorms, thick fog, and violent wind are common.
- Barbarian tribes (including [[Tribe of the Owl]]) roam the tundra and trade periodically with Ten-Towns.
- Reghed barbarians of Reghed descent are found in the area around [[Caer Konig]].
