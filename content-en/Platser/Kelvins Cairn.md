---
title: "Kelvin's Cairn"
---

- **Type:** Enormous mountain peak, dominating the horizon north of [[Caer Konig]].
- **The Fortress (eastern face):** [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s base, 5 miles (~5 hrs) from Caer Konig. Contained cells with fungus-infected prisoners (up to 375 days), an alchemy lab with Underdark mushroom, and a separate bunker with ale storage. Defeated by [[Zahirs gäng]] on Day 18.
- A stream flows out of the fortress — may partly function as a dam for a water reservoir inside the mountain.
- **South of the mountain (Day 26):** Abandoned dog sled found with thin but living dogs — [[Garret]]'s sled. Lead dog [[Boy(†)]] guided the gang up the mountain. Boy was found frozen in a blizzard on Day 28.
- **Up the mountain (Day 26):** An avalanche swept [[Zahirs gäng]] approximately 1,000 feet down. The gang found [[Garret]] in the snow — alive but badly injured after a yeti attack higher up. Two crag cats using Garret as bait attacked and were defeated.
- **The cave system (Day 26):** Blood and yeti tracks at a cave. A plateau above: collapsed tent, [[Asterix(†)]] frozen solid and stone-dead, a headless dwarf. Inside the cave: a large chamber with a mother yeti and cub — [[Perilou Fishfinger]] was held captive as a "toy." [[Mokingo Akannathi(†)]] found dead inside. Perilou rescued by an invisible [[Sork the Shadow]].
- **Yetis:** Father yeti killed outside the cave by [[Zahirs gäng]] (Day 26). Mother yeti still alive — never crossed the wooden bridge and is likely still on the mountain.
- **Garret** rescued and returned to [[Caer Konig]] (Day 28). [[The Burning Hammers]]: Mokingo and Asterix dead, Perilou rescued.
