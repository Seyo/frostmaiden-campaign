---
title: "Auril"
---

Goddess of cold, winter, and death — the *Frostmaiden*. Has placed a curse over [[Icewind Dale]] that blocks the sun and creates eternal winter.

## The Curse

- The sun no longer shines over [[Icewind Dale]].
- Light spells do not function in the region.
- [[Ten-Towns]] offers food and refrains from lighting fires to appease her.

## Worshippers

- [[Tribe of the Owl]] worships [[Uthgar]], not Auril directly — but their relationship to her in this winter is unclear.
- Suspected cultists in [[Ten-Towns]] may be behind the murders (see [[Morden i Ten-Towns]]).

## Connections to current events

- **The ice pick killer:** victims are marked with a snowflake carved into the chest (most recently in [[Termalain]] on days 19–23). The pattern points to Auril ritual magic or a cult in her name.
- **[[Zahir]]'s preaching:** claims that life in [[Ten-Towns]] was better when humans were sacrificed to Auril. The claim is historically unverified and politically explosive ahead of the town council.

> Auril's own avatars or direct agents have not yet been identified in the campaign. Update on contact.
