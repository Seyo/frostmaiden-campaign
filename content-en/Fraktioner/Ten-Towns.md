---
title: "Ten-Towns"
---

The collective name for the ten settlements of Icewind Dale. Each is governed locally with sheriffs, and a council meets in [[Bryn Shander]].

![[ten-towns.webp]]

**All ten towns:**
- [[Bryn Shander]] — Largest, council hall with sheriffs, packed with refugees.
- [[Caer Konig]] — Small, on the shore of [[Lac Dinneshere]], ruins of an old castle.
- [[Caer Dineval]] — Passed through heading north (fines paid). On [[Lac Dinneshere]].
- Easthaven — Halfling hunter murdered here (ice-pick killing).
- Targos — Human shipbuilder murdered here (ice-pick killing).
- Bremen — On the Shaengarne River.
- Lonelywood — Forest town on Maer Dualdon.
- Termalaine — On Maer Dualdon.
- Dougan's Hole — On Redwaters.
- Good Mead — On Redwaters.

**Other:**
- The towns make offerings to [[Auril]] (food, or refraining from lighting fires) to appease her.
- All of [[Zahirs gäng]] are registered on a sacrifice list in [[Bryn Shander]].
