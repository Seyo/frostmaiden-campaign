Isolated dwarf community in [[Dvärgadalen]], ~8–10 hours by dog sled from [[Caer Konig]]. Likely unconnected to the [[Sunblight-familjen]] (different type of dwarf).

- Highly isolated and independent — distrustful of strangers.
- Trade in gemstones.
- Trading partners with [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] and [[Eevie]] via the guide [[Jarthra]].
- [[Glenn]] at *The Hook Line and Sinker* imports ale from here — one of their few ties to the outside world.

> The party has not visited Dvärgadalen. All information is second-hand.
