---
title: "Boy"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Lead dog in [[Garret]]'s sled team. Hero without a title.

- Found tied to an overturned sled south of [[Kelvins Cairn]] (Day 26) — thin, panicked, but alive. Fed by the gang.
- [[Zahir]] spoke to Boy via *Speak with Animals*: completely fixated on finding his "Master." Confirmed that [[Garret]] was up the mountain.
- Unhitched and led the gang up Kelvin's Cairn through biting cold and brutal terrain.
- Reunited with [[Garret]] after the gang defeated the two crag cats that had used Garret as bait. Very happy.
