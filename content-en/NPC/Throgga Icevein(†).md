---
title: "Throgga Icevein†"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Bryn Shander) — **† UNDEAD**

Apparent owner of *Throgga's Import and Goods* in [[Bryn Shander]]. In reality an **undead** — controlled by an unknown power since the Rime began.

- Hired [[Zahirs gäng]] (via [[Aubril(†)]]) to escort goods from [[Fireshear]] to Bryn Shander.
- Was displeased about Aubril's death but still paid out half the fee (37.5 gp) after [[Zahir]] negotiated. Called the party "Zahir's crew." Asked the party to seek out [[Hildur Trollbane]].
- Was present in [[Termalaine]] (Day 34) — a delivery coinciding with the murders.
- **Revealed as undead (Day 34):** [[Nasher]] cast Divine Sense and immediately sensed that Throgga was not alive. Confronted and attacked the party without a word.
- Dropped by [[Sork the Shadow]] with Shadow Blades. A **translucent white wraith** rose from the body and continued fighting — terrorized the party — before being felled by Sork.
- Throgga's body decayed rapidly to a skeleton. Nasher estimated he had been **dead for approximately 2.5 years** — exactly since the Rime began and [[Icewind Dale]] fell into darkness.

**Connection to the murder investigation:** Deliveries from Throgga's Import and Goods coincided with the serial murders in each town. The killers likely used him as cover for their travels.

**Employees (suspected killers):** Magnar, Fanny Chestnut, Sirus (red tiefling) + a **bald human** (bodyguard who fled during the confrontation in Termalaine and vanished without a trace).
