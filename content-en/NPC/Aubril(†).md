---
title: "Aubril†"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Bryn Shander)

Merchant, employed by *Throgga's Import and Goods*. Hired [[Zahirs gäng]] for 75 gp to escort him and his cargo from [[Fireshear]] to [[Bryn Shander]].

Killed by wolves at the border of Icewind Dale (Day 6–7). His body was left in pieces. [[Nasher]] stopped [[Zahir]] from blowing up the corpse with magic — instead his belongings were transported back to [[Throgga Icevein(†)]]. See [[Dödsloggen]].
