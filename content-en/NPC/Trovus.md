---
title: "Trovus"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

White dragonborn, self-appointed night watchman of [[Caer Konig]]. Drunk and incompetent — seems to cause more harm than good. Background unknown; refuses to talk about where he comes from.

- Introduced the party to [[Cori]] and [[Allie]] at *The Northern Light*.
- Provided partially incorrect information: said the murder victim was a dwarf glassblower — turned out to be [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]].
- When confronted about his incompetence, he had no answers.
