title: "Asterix†"
---

**Faction:** [[The Burning Hammers]]

Female tiefling, one of three members of [[The Burning Hammers]].

- Found stone-dead and frozen solid on a plateau on [[Kelvins Cairn]] (Day 26) — blank, glassy eyes. Exact circumstances unknown. Beside her body lay a headless dwarf (killed long before her arrival with a miner's pick).
- Carried: a **potion of invisibility** (used by [[Sork the Shadow]] to rescue [[Perilou Fishfinger]]) and a **leather-bound spellbook** (possibly trapped — sold by [[Zahir]] for 175 gp in [[Termalaine]]).
- The tent at the plateau had been ransacked long ago.
