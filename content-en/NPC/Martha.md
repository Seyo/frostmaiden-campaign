---
title: "Martha"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Termalaine)

Innkeeper at **The East Side Inn** in [[Termalaine]]. Close friend of [[Keegan Valryn]] and [[Garret]].

- The East Side Inn consists of several interconnected buildings via underground tunnels. No open fires allowed in the rooms.
- Gave [[Zahirs gäng]] free lodging for a week (Days 28–34) as thanks for what they did for Keegan and Garret.
- Told the party (Day 34) that the murdered mine worker was named [[Fjordis Jewelhand]]. See [[Morden i Ten-Towns]].
