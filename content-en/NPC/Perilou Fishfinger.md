---
title: "Perilou Fishfinger"
---

**Faction:** Independent (former [[The Burning Hammers]])

Halfling, cleric of **Yondalla** — the matriarch, goddess of protection and fertility. Lawful good. Yondalla's symbol: a cornucopia on a shield.

- Was part of [[The Burning Hammers]] (led by her husband [[Mokingo Akannathi(†)]]) heading toward [[Termalaine]] to investigate the murders.
- Captured by the mother yeti while exploring the cave system on [[Kelvins Cairn]] (Day 26) — kept alive as a "toy" for the cub.
- Rescued by an invisible [[Sork the Shadow]] who crept past the yetis, gave her a healing potion, and led her out along a rocky ledge.
- Granted the party Yondalla's blessing during the battle against the father yeti.
- Revealed on Day 28: all murder victims have **a snowflake carved into their left shoulder**; the murders coincided with deliveries from [[Throgga Icevein(†)|Throgga's Import & Goods]].
- Mokingo was her husband → devastated by his death.
- Refuses to adventure further after the mountain ordeal. Transported to [[Termalaine]] by [[Jarthra]]. Currently in Termalaine.
