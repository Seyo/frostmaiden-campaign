**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

[[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]]'s wife. Kidnapped by [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s dwarves the same night he was murdered, taken to the fortress at [[Kelvins Cairn]].

- In the fortress cell block she was infected with fungus as part of the Sunblight family's alchemical process.
- **Day 18:** [[Nasher]] opened her cell in an attempt to save her; the infected Eevie attacked and was cut down in self-defense. See [[Nashers misstag]] and [[Dödsloggen]]. Nasher wrapped her in his own blanket and carried her out with reverence — he shed a tear, the first time since Letharia was executed.
- **Day 19:** The gang brought the body back to Caer Konig. The following day it was taken to [[Foxes in the Snow]], who confirmed Eevie's condition was caused by Duergar spores from the Underdark.
