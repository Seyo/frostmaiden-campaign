---
title: "Eevie"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

[[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]]'s wife. Disappeared the same night he was murdered — likely kidnapped by [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s dwarves.

- A creature was found in the fortress cell block with blonde hair, a small frame, and a white nightgown — possibly Eevie, but infected with fungus.
- [[Nasher]] opened the cell in an attempt to save her; the creature attacked and [[Nasher]] cut it down in self-defense.
- **Unknown:** Whether it truly was Eevie, and whether she could have been saved. See [[Nashers misstag]].
