**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Half-elf, runs *The Hook, Line and Sinker* in [[Caer Konig]]. Unusually pleasant and welcoming — the only genuinely friendly person the party has met in Icewind Dale. Sells expensive cheese and imports ale from the dwarves of [[Dvärgadalen]].

- [[The Burning Hammers]] stayed with him one night before they disappeared.
- Something seems off — nobody is this friendly in this frozen hellhole.
