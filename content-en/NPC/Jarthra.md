---
title: "Jarthra"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Elf with dark hair in a simple braid, guide, runs *Frozen-fur Expeditions* in [[Caer Konig]]. Helped [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] and [[Eevie(†)]] trade with the dwarves of [[Dvärgadalen]] (~8–10 hrs by dog sled).

- Guided [[Zahirs gäng]] to [[Kelvins Cairn]] for 17 gp.
- Believes the killer came from outside the town — supports the Sunblight-dwarf theory.
- [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] belonged to the Tribe of the Owl; Jarthra knew this and only revealed it under pressure.
- Her pearls stolen by the thieves — recovered in the fortress. When returned (Day 19) she rewarded the gang with a **Hood of Water Breathing**, gemstones, and a promised future favour.
- Pragmatic about [[Glenn]]'s ale dealings with the Sunblight dwarves — "up here you have to do business with everyone to survive."
- Discussed old Auril sacrifices with [[Zahir]] on the journey back from the fortress (Day 18–19) — believes life was better when people were offered up to [[Auril]].
- Guided the gang for free (redeeming the promised favour) toward [[Termalain]] via the wilderness north of [[Dvärgadalen]] (Day 24). Stayed at the abandoned dog sled camp south of [[Kelvins Cairn]] while the gang climbed to rescue [[Garret]].
