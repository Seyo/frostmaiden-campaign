title: "Mokingo Akannathi†"
---

**Faction:** [[The Burning Hammers]]

Goliath, leader of [[The Burning Hammers]]. Known as "Growling Bear." Husband of [[Perilou Fishfinger]].

- Led [[The Burning Hammers]] to [[Kelvins Cairn]] to search for treasure (his idea, not the guide [[Garret]]'s).
- Was attacked by a yeti in the cave system — fought the yeti and in doing so saved [[Garret]].
- Found dead in the yeti cave system by [[Sork the Shadow]] during the rescue of [[Perilou Fishfinger]] (Day 26).
