title: "Foxes in the Snow"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Older human woman, runs the alchemy shop *Snow Fox Hideout* in [[Caer Konig]]. Healer, reclusive, dislikes magic and strangers. Keeps two owls as pets and spies.

- Stopped a break-in attempt with a homemade trap (Tasha's Hideous Laughter). The intruder (male voice) fled.
- Her owls tracked the footprints all the way to the eastern face of [[Kelvins Cairn]] — a crucial lead for the party.
- Received [[Eevie(†)]]'s fungus-infected body the day after the gang returned to Caer Konig (Day 19).
- Confirmed that the dwarves in the fortress were not ordinary dwarves — they were **Duergar from the Underdark**.
- Healed [[Nasher]]'s scratch from Eevie with magic. Gave him an herbal drink to take during the week (tasted awful — seemed to work).
- Advised the gang to leave the mushroom in Nildar's laboratory where they found it — the right call.
