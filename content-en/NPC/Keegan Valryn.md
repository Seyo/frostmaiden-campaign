---
title: "Keegan Valryn"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Dog sled rental operator in [[Caer Konig]], married to the guide [[Garret]]. Disappeared with a dog sled on Day 17 — went out searching for [[Garret]] and [[The Burning Hammers]].

- Returned to [[Caer Konig]] during Days 19–23 without having found [[Garret]].
- Hired [[Zahirs gäng]] to rescue Garret: reward **50 gp** + free loan of a dog sled.
- [[Garret]] was rescued by the gang on [[Kelvins Cairn]] on Day 26 — alive but badly injured after a yeti attack.
