Grey-hooded dwarf, older and better equipped than his subordinates. Led the fortress at [[Kelvins Cairn]]. Claimed all of Icewind Dale belongs to the Sunblight family.

- **Crimes:** Sent dwarves to murder [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]], kidnap [[Eevie(†)]] (who died in the fortress cell block), and steal from [[Caer Konig]]. Infected prisoners with fungus (alchemical process).
- **Defeated** by [[Zahirs gäng]] on Day 18. Zahir left him rolling on the floor in laughter, Sork filled him with arrows.
- **Last words:** *"My father rides on a dragon's back and shall destroy all of Ten-Towns."*
- See [[Sunblight-familjen]] for the continuing threat.
