---
title: "The Burning Hammers"
---

**Faction:** Independent

A group of three adventurers who came to [[Caer Konig]] to investigate the murder of [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] and [[Eevie(†)]]'s disappearance. Guided out by [[Garret]] toward [[Termalaine]].

**Members:**
- **[[Mokingo Akannathi(†)]]** – goliath, "Growling Bear," leader. Husband of Perilou. Killed by a yeti in the cave system on [[Kelvins Cairn]] (Day 26).
- **[[Perilou Fishfinger]]** – halfling, cleric of Yondalla, lawful good. Rescued from the yeti cave by [[Sork the Shadow]] (Day 26). Refuses to adventure further. Currently in [[Termalaine]].
- **[[Asterix(†)]]** – female tiefling. Found stone-dead and frozen on a plateau on [[Kelvins Cairn]] (Day 26).

- Stayed one night at *The Hook Line and Sinker* ([[Glenn]]).
- Left [[Caer Konig]] 4–5 days before [[Zahirs gäng]] arrived, guided out by [[Garret]].
- [[Keegan Valryn]] hired [[Zahirs gäng]] to search for them — 50 gp reward.
