---
title: "The Burning Hammers"
---

**Faction:** Independent

A group of three adventurers who came to [[Caer Konig]] to investigate the murder of [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] and [[Eevie(†)]]'s disappearance. Appeared to be average adventurers.

- Stayed one night at *The Hook Line and Sinker* ([[Glenn]]).
- Left town 4–5 days before [[Zahirs gäng]] arrived, guided out by [[Garret]].
- [[Keegan Valryn]] hired [[Zahirs gäng]] to search for them — 50 gp reward.
- [[Garret]] was found alive on [[Kelvins Cairn]] (Day 26) and reported that he and The Burning Hammers were attacked by a **yeti** higher up the mountain. Garret barely escaped. The Burning Hammers' fate unknown.
- **Status:** Missing, presumed dead.
