**Faction:** [[Tribe of the Owl]], [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Human barbarian from the Tribe of the Owl, settled in [[Caer Konig]] as a blacksmith (*Snowy Baubles*). Married [[Eevie]]. Had a hot temper — only Eevie could calm him.

- **Murdered** in his sleep (throat slit) by [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s dwarves. See [[Dödsloggen]].
- Crime scene: the bedroom of *Snowy Baubles*. A white lock of hair (likely from the killer's beard) found at the body.
- Valuable gems (~20 gp) in an iron chest left untouched — not a robbery, a targeted killing.
- Connected to [[Uthgar]] (a barbarian-clan deity statue found in the home).
