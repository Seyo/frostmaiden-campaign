**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Guide in [[Caer Konig]], married to [[Keegan Valryn]]. Led [[The Burning Hammers]] out of town and guided them toward Termalain through the wilderness north of [[Dvärgadalen]].

- Attacked by a **yeti** higher up on [[Kelvins Cairn]] together with [[The Burning Hammers]] (Day 26). Barely escaped. The others' fate unknown.
- Found in the snow on the mountain by Zahir's gang — alive but badly injured, bloodied and in shock (Day 26). Healed by [[Nasher]].
- His lead sled dog **Boy** was reunited with him after Zahir spoke to the dog via *Speak with Animals* and the gang tracked their way up the mountain with Boy leading the way.
- Rescued — but the gang is still up on the mountain at the session's end.
