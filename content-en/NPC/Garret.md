---
title: "Garret"
---

**Faction:** [[Ten-Towns]] (Caer Konig)

Guide in [[Caer Konig]], married to [[Keegan Valryn]]. Led [[The Burning Hammers]] out of town and guided them toward Termalaine through the wilderness north of [[Dvärgadalen]].

- Attacked by a **yeti** higher up on [[Kelvins Cairn]] together with [[The Burning Hammers]] (Day 26). Barely escaped — [[Mokingo Akannathi(†)]] fought the yeti and in doing so saved Garret.
- Found in the snow on the mountain by Zahir's gang — alive but badly injured, bloodied and in shock (Day 26). Healed by [[Nasher]].
- His lead sled dog **[[Boy(†)]]** was reunited with him on the mountain — later found frozen to death (Day 28). Garret was devastated.
- Hid during the battle against the yetis (Day 26) — helped the gang set up camp on the plateau afterward.
- Returned to [[Caer Konig]] and [[Keegan Valryn]] (Day 28) when the party continued toward [[Termalaine]].
