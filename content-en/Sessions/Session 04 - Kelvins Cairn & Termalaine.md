# Session 4 – Kelvins Cairn & Termalaine

**Day 26 (continued) – Kelvins Cairn:**

*The Burning Hammers:*
- Zahir identified the crag cats as "Hunters of Men" — common in the mountains.
- [[Garret]] described [[The Burning Hammers]]: leader [[Mokingo Akannathi(†)]] (goliath, "Growling Bear"), [[Perilou Fishfinger]] (halfling, devotee of Yondalla, lawful good), and [[Asterix(†)]] (female tiefling). Garret served as guide; originally from the Tribe of the Elk.

*The Cave and the Plateau:*
- Climbed the cliff face. Found blood and yeti tracks at a cave — the tracks continued further up the mountain.
- Called into the cave → yeti growled → backed out.
- Climbed to a plateau: collapsed tent, snowdrift, blue boots. Found [[Asterix(†)]] stone-dead and frozen solid — blank, glassy eyes. Beside her: a headless dwarf (killed long ago with a miner's pick).
- Asterix carried: a potion of invisibility + a leather-bound spellbook (possibly trapped — Zahir pocketed it without opening it).
- Borc took the dwarf's blue boots. The tent had been ransacked long ago — left everything.

*Perilou Rescued:*
- Re-entered the cave, crossed the ice floor on skates. The tunnel opened into a large chamber.
- Below on a ledge: a yeti mother with her cub — the cub was playing with a living halfling: **[[Perilou Fishfinger]]**.
- Sork drank the invisibility potion, crept past the yetis, gave Perilou a healing potion, and led her out along a rocky ledge.
- Found the dead [[Mokingo Akannathi(†)]] and a dwarf head along the way out.

*Battle Against the Father Yeti:*
- Outside the cave: the father yeti holding a dead mountain goat. Attempts to scare him off failed.
- Zahir (Tasha's Hideous Laughter), Nasher (Tyr's justice), Perilou (Yondalla's blessing), Sork (Shadow Blades) → father yeti killed. No losses.

*The Chaotic Retreat:*
- The mother came out of the cave — the party was forced to flee across a narrow wooden bridge.
- Borc fell → Nasher dragged him. Sork encased in an ice block → broke free → the mother knocked Sork unconscious. Perilou hit by an ice block → fell unconscious (fortunately already on the safe side of the bridge).
- Nasher ran back across and dragged Sork. Zahir stabilized Borc and Sork (Spare the Dying), hurled Eldritch Blasts at the mother. Everyone evacuated. The mother never crossed the bridge — too narrow.
- Garret had hidden throughout the entire fight.
- Camped on the plateau — impossible to descend with three unconscious companions. Garret accidentally burned the food; Zahir ruined the drinks in the brewing kit. Zahir set an alarm spell. Nasher kept watch. Quiet night.

**Day 27:**
- None of the unconscious woke up → forced to spend another night. Food nearly gone — Garret and Perilou ate from the party's rations. Managed to secure food and drink during the night.

**Day 28:**

*Perilou Speaks:*
- Everyone finally woke.
- All murder victims had **a snowflake carved into their left shoulder**.
- The murders coincided with **deliveries to the towns from [[Throgga Icevein(†)|Throgga's Import & Goods]]** — strong connection.
- Mokingo was Perilou's husband → she is devastated.

*The Descent:*
- Fog and difficult climbing down the cliff.
- Sork spotted a massive blizzard approaching → dug a snow shelter with their shovels → the storm passed.
- Borc mentioned that a similar storm had separated him and his crew after the shipwreck.

*Boy Found:*
- Found **[[Boy(†)]]** frozen to death where they had left him. [[Garret]] was devastated.
- If they hadn't burned the food and ruined the drinks on the first night on the mountain, they could have made it down in time.

*Heading to Termalaine:*
- Said their goodbyes to [[Jarthra]] and Garret. Garret headed back to [[Caer Konig]] and [[Keegan Valryn]].
- Perilou won't adventure anymore — wants to go to Termalaine.
- [[Jarthra]] drove the dogs hard to Termalaine. Wolves howled in the distance.

*Arrival in Termalaine (late Day 28):*
- Mining town along [[Maer Dualdon]], roughly 600 inhabitants. Mines crystals and diamonds. Sacrifices warmth to [[Auril]] — freezes willingly.
- **The East Side Inn**: innkeeper **[[Martha]]**, close friend of [[Keegan Valryn]] and [[Garret]]. Interconnected buildings via underground tunnels — no open fires allowed in rooms. Free lodging for a week.
- **Speaker Oarus Matthews** — the town's leader, missing for ~a week. Last seen since a murder of a mine worker.

**Days 29–33 – Investigation in Termalaine:**
- Nasher: attempted to make contacts (carousing, middle class) → failed.
- Zahir: sold Asterix's spellbook → **175 gp**.
- Borc: looked for work → high unemployment. Learned:
  - New mine closed for 2 weeks — monsters came up from underground.
  - Speaker last seen at **The Blue Clam** — got drunk, vanished.
- Sork: investigated the Speaker. **Elka Verryt** (secretary) may know where he is. Staff trapped in the closed new mine: foreman **Fronda Harroway** (dwarf), lieutenants **Menka Bladeless** (half-elf) and **Oval Chon** (human), other workers **Brin Ruk'Natsk** (race unknown), **Bezeuth von Deeb** (dwarf), **Mari Flatstream** (genasi), **Sod Suscagan** (half-orc), **Gror Gersk** (race unknown).
- Fronda's adopted son **Arno** — mute, orphaned (Tribe of the Wolf), somewhere in town.
- Rumour: **Gillian Fieldspar** (dwarf jeweller, "Fieldspar", from Dwarven Valley) — shop closed for 8 days.
- Murder during the week: mine worker **Fjordis Jewelhand**.
- Nasher traded his Greatsword for a **Longsword + sturdy shield** (Tyr's symbol painted on). Sork traded for a map of the new mine.

**Day 34 – Throgga Revealed:**

*Murder Investigation:*
- Gravedigger **Crank** (gnome): Fjordis had stab and slash wounds (3 total) + a snowflake on the shoulder (frost-burned in, not branded). First Termalaine victim: **Bromm Runehand** (found at the harbour, 9 days ago).
- Mine workers' collective: silver-scaled dragonborn **Heskam Wyrmhide** told them about Fjordis (died 4 days ago, body with Crank).

*Confronting [[Throgga Icevein(†)]]:*
- Throgga was in Termalaine (delivery coinciding with the murders).
- Throgga claimed to have arrived with: **Magnar**, **Fanny Chestnut**, and **Sirus** (red tiefling) — hired in Bryn Shander. Claimed not to know where they were.
- A drunk at **The Blue Clam** said Throgga actually had **five** with him: four dwarves, a tiefling, and a **bald human**.
- Nasher confronted Throgga (Booming Voice) → no reaction. Cast **Divine Sense** → **Throgga is undead**.
- _"You cannot fool a holy paladin of Tyr. I know exactly what you are. Undead. Speak now."_ → Throgga attacked.

*The Battle:*
- Sork quickly dropped Throgga with Shadow Blades.
- A **translucent white wraith** rose from the body — terrorized the party (Nasher and Sork briefly affected; Borc held his ground but was then dropped by a single blow).
- During the fight: Nasher spotted a **bald man** (Throgga's bodyguard, recognized from Bryn Shander) flee around a corner.
- Sork felled the wraith with Shadow Blades. The party gave chase → Sork tracked footprints → the trail vanished without a trace.

*The Revelation:*
- Throgga's body decayed rapidly to a skeleton. Nasher: Throgga had been **dead for approximately 2.5 years** — since the Rime began.
- The killers are likely Throgga's employees: Magnar, Fanny Chestnut, Sirus, and the bald man.
- **[[Hildur Trollbane]] was right.** Recovered the gold paid for the climbing gear + **17 gp** from the skeleton.
