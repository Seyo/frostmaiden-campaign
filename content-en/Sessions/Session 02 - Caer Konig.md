# Session 2 – Caer Konig: The Fortress at Kelvins Cairn

**Day 16 (cont.) – Arrival:**
- The dog sled was left with [[Keegan Valryn]] (rental operator). Directed to *The Northern Light*.
- Met [[Trovus]] (white dragonborn, drunk) outside *The Hook, Line and Sinker*. He reported:
  - Murder of [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] and disappearance of his wife [[Eevie]] (not a dwarf glassblower as Hildur had said — incorrect information).
  - Invisible thieves — including the stolen magic lantern from *The Northern Light*.
  - [[The Burning Hammers]] (3 adventurers) left 4–5 days ago, not heard from since.
- Introduced to [[Cori]] and [[Allie]] at *The Northern Light*. Cori offered free lodging in exchange for help.

**Day 17 – Investigation:**
- [[Borc the Orc]] heard loud laughter (Tasha's Hideous Laughter) from the south during the night watch, woke [[Zahir]]. Tracked footprints to *Snow Fox Hideout*.
- Visited [[Foxes in the Snow]]: a break-in attempt stopped by her trap the previous night, the intruder (male voice) fled. Nothing stolen. Offered her owls to track.
- Footprints led south along the shore of [[Lac Dinneshere]] and vanished into the fog beyond the village.
- Examined the crime scene at *Snowy Baubles* (Bronn's home) with [[Trovus]]:
  - [[Bronn Bearhammer(†)]] murdered in his sleep, throat slit. White lock of hair (beard?) near the body.
  - Iron chest with broken lock — valuable gems (~20 gp) still inside. Missing scrimshaw decoration.
  - Eevie's side of the bed in disarray — likely kidnapped. A wooden statue of [[Uthgar]] found.
- Visited [[Jarthra]] (elf, guide, *Frozen Expeditions*):
  - Bronn belonged to the Tribe of the Owl. The tribe visits about once a month.
  - Helped Bronn and Eevie trade with the dwarves in [[Dvärgadalen]] (~8–10 hrs by dog sled).
  - Believes the killer came from outside the village. Her own pearls stolen by the thieves.
- [[Keegan Valryn]] went missing with a dog sled during the day — likely searching for [[Garret]] and The Burning Hammers.
- *The Hook, Line and Sinker*: Met [[Glenn]] (half-elf, innkeeper, sells expensive cheese, imports ale from the dwarves). Something seems off about him.
- Confronted [[Trovus]] about his incompetence — he had no answers.
- Night watch uneventful.

**Day 18 – The Fortress at Kelvins Cairn:**
- [[Foxes in the Snow]] sought out the party — her owls had tracked footprints to the eastern face of [[Kelvins Cairn]], 5 miles away.
- Hired [[Jarthra]] as guide for 17 gp. Bought provisions. Set out with 2 dog sleds.
- [[Sork the Shadow]] lost the trail (~2 hrs extra). Around 16:00: stone fortress in the mountain's foot with light showing.
- Left sleds ~200 m out. Climbed up from a ravine with a grappling hook.
- [[Sork the Shadow]] scouted the bunker: grey-hooded dwarf, large white beard, crossbow, scale armour → match with hair lock from Bronn's bedroom and the small footprints.
- Slipped in through the heavy double doors ([[Borc the Orc]] hauled them open). Inside: a trapdoor, 3 iron cages (stolen goats, an undead creature, empty).
  - **South corridor:** A dwarf spotted the party. Combat — the dwarf magically enlarged himself. Defeated, bound in a cage. Sork took a battle axe wound.
  - **West corridor (cell block):** 4 cells with fungus-covered humanoid creatures. One possible [[Eevie]] (blonde hair, small, white nightgown). [[Nasher]] opened the cell — the creature attacked — [[Nasher]] cut it down in self-defense. Whether it was truly Eevie remains unknown.
  - A dwarf tried to open all the cells as an alarm — [[Borc the Orc]] teleported and stopped him.
  - **North corridor:** Older, better-equipped dwarf at a lever — [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]. Spoke Dwarvish. Claimed Icewind Dale belongs to the Sunblight family. Offered peace for 15 years if the party left.
    - [[Nasher]] refused. Combat: [[Sork the Shadow]] landed an arrow. [[Borc the Orc]] teleported straight into the spike trap (Nildar pulled the lever) — dropped to 0 hp, stabilized by [[Zahir]]. Unconscious at session's end. [[Nasher]] knocked out by a misty substance (a thrown vial). [[Zahir]] finished off [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]] with a spell; [[Sork the Shadow]] filled him with arrows.
    - **Last words:** *"My father rides on a dragon's back and shall destroy all of Ten-Towns."*
- In Nildar's quarters: coins, a magic lantern (stolen from *The Northern Light*), an alchemy laboratory.
- Stolen goods recovered on the upper floor, including [[Jarthra]]'s pearls.
