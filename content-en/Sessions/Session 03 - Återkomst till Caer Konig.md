# Session 3 – Return to Caer Konig

**Day 18 (cont.) – Morning at the Fortress:**
- A 1-hour short rest to bring the gang back to life. When the rest ends, the bound dwarf from the south corridor has broken free.
- [[Sork the Shadow]] sees **two pairs of invisible footprints** leaving the fortress — someone (also invisible) has helped the dwarf escape. [[Sork the Shadow]] and [[Nasher]] give chase but cannot catch up.
- 1 hour searching the fortress:
  - Signs above each cell show how many days the captive has been held. The most developed mushroom creature has been caged for **375 days**.
  - [[Borc the Orc]] finds, among other things, a box of **tiny crystals** in the packing area — a suspected match for the missing crystals from [[Eevie(†)]]'s scrimshaw work at *Snowy Baubles* (unconfirmed).
  - [[Zahir]] finds an alchemy lab with a **glowing mushroom** in an uncapped glass flask in [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s quarters. Leaves it alone — later confirmed by [[Foxes in the Snow]] as the right call: the spores likely come from the **Underdark** and are dangerous to inhale.
  - In the south room (where they fought the first dwarf), [[Nasher]] finds a **trapdoor in the floor** leading to a bunker outside the fortress, and a room full of **ale barrels**.
  - [[Nasher]] finds a **business ledger** proving that [[Glenn]] (*The Hook, Line and Sinker*) has been buying ale from the Sunblight dwarves for **roughly 2 years**. Nasher confronts [[Jarthra]] directly: it turns out she has not bought ale from these dwarves, and stopped delivering to Glenn about 2 years ago — matching the ledger. [[Jarthra]] takes a pragmatic view of Glenn — up here you have to do business with everyone to survive.
  - The **undead creature** in its cage: [[Sork the Shadow]] voices that he doesn't like that it's still alive — but the gang is distracted and the creature is left behind.
  - **Forgotten:** the goats in their cages.
- [[Nasher]] takes his blanket from his pack, wraps [[Eevie(†)]] in it, and carries her out with reverence. It is the first time he has shed a tear since Letharia was executed.
- The body is carried out and placed in the sled with full dignity. [[Jarthra]] wants to go home.

**The journey back:**
- On the way back, [[Zahir]] and [[Jarthra]] discuss the old sacrifices to [[Auril]] — was life better when people were still being offered up to her? [[Jarthra]] believes it was. [[Nasher]] finds the thought of sacrificing humans to evil utterly horrifying. [[Zahir]] seems genuinely curious rather than merely provocative.
- The gang sets off back to Caer Konig **in the middle of the night**.

**Day 19 – Arrival in Caer Konig and rest:**
- They arrive extremely early in the day.
- [[Nasher]] suddenly: *"DAMMIT! WE FORGOT THE GOATS!"* — the realization hits all the way back in Caer Konig.
- [[Eevie(†)]]'s body is left to the townsfolk.
- [[Jarthra]]'s pearls are returned — she is overjoyed and rewards the gang:
  - **Hood of Water Breathing** (an old cowl Jarthra received in a trade; she suspected it was magical but didn't know its effect)
  - Various valuable gemstones
  - **A promise of future help** — cashed in later in the session to hire [[Jarthra]] as a guide
- The magic lantern (found in Nildar's quarters during session 2) is returned to [[Cori & Allie]] at *The Northern Light* — free lodging for five days for the whole gang.
- The day after returning, the gang takes [[Eevie(†)]]'s fungus-infected body to [[Foxes in the Snow]]:
  - Foxes confirms that the dwarves they encountered at the fortress were not ordinary dwarves — they were **Duergar from the Underdark**.
  - Foxes heals [[Nasher]]'s scratch wound from Eevie with magic and gives him an herbal drink to take during the week. The taste is truly awful, but it seems to work.

**Days 19–23 – Rest and rumors in Caer Konig:**
- Five days of rest and crafting at *The Northern Light*.
- [[Sork the Shadow]] and [[Borc the Orc]] pick up odd jobs around town — scrape together **16.5 gp** combined.
- [[Nasher]] preaches about his god, Tyr, to the townsfolk. The sermons seem appreciated.
- [[Zahir]] spends his evenings drinking with [[Trovus]] on a couple of occasions.
- Over evenings at the tavern, [[Nasher]] learns more about the others:
  - **[[Zahir]]**: Early forties. Claims he "made a mistake" in Calimport and had to flee. "No one is chasing him." Wants to return to Calimport but must wait for things to blow over. Won't go into detail. Nasher doesn't trust him.
  - **[[Sork the Shadow]]**: Was a fixer in Waterdeep for the nobleman **Lord Cassalanter** — a name Nasher doesn't recognize. What Sork did is unclear — criminal or legitimate? Cassalanter was corrupt and abused his underlings. Sork held a dagger to the nobleman's throat but didn't kill him. Had to flee Waterdeep when things fell apart. Nasher tried to steer Sork toward Tyr's path. Similar history and values.
  - **[[Borc the Orc]]**: Still a quiet mystery. But his heart seems to be in the right place.
- [[Zahir]] identifies during the week:
  - **Hood of Water Breathing**: speak a command word while wearing it and you are enclosed in a bubble that lets you breathe normally underwater.
  - **Potion of Healing** (found in the fortress).
- [[Nasher]]'s scratch from Eevie heals cleanly by the end of the rest — Foxes' herbal drink seems to have done its job.
- **New murder reported:** A human fisherman in [[Termalain]] was found with a **snowflake carved into his chest**. The murder took place **the day before the gang arrived in Caer Konig** (Day 15) — but they only learn of it during the rest. The pattern matches the ice pick killer. [[Garret]] and [[The Burning Hammers]] were heading that way.
- **[[Borc the Orc]]** took [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]]'s breastplate and put it on — it fits surprisingly well.

**Late in the stay – [[Keegan Valryn]] returns:**
- [[Keegan Valryn]] returns to Caer Konig and gives the gang a **quest**: find his husband [[Garret]] (the guide who went out with [[The Burning Hammers]]).
- Reward: **50 gp** + free loan of a dog sled.
- Preparations: sells off fortress loot, buys provisions, hires [[Jarthra]] as guide using the promised future help.

**Day 24 – Departure toward [[Termalain]] – the route north of [[Dvärgadalen]]:**
- The weather is dreadful — a heavy snowstorm with terrible visibility. The gang buys a few pairs of goggles. [[Jarthra]] leads.
- The route north of Dvärgadalen, south of [[Kelvins Cairn]] is slow going. Zahir and Borc seem to be taking it especially badly.
- A **cave with a faint yellow glow** appears — shelter from the storm. Zahir and Sork sneak in; Zahir hears **Dwarvish** from deeper inside.
- The whole gang is fetched and [[Nasher]] walks straight up to introduce himself. Three **friendly dwarves**: **Hruna, Korux, and Storn**. Not Duergar — ordinary dwarves transporting a wagon of iron ore toward Brynn Shander. A **yeti** killed one of their companions and goblins stole the wagon. The gang agrees to help.

**Tracking — yeti, goblins, and polar bears:**
- Tracks lead out from the cave. The dead dwarf is found — torn in two, yeti attack, blood everywhere. Nothing Nasher can do. Around the body: **tiny footprints and wagon tracks**.
- [[Sork the Shadow]]: *"It's definitely goblins."* — It was goblins.
- The gang sneaks ahead and sees:
  - **4 goblins** pulling the stolen dwarf wagon.
  - **3 goblins** (a leader with thicker armor and better weapons, two foot soldiers) near a **large goblin wagon with a roof and spiked stakes**.
  - **2 genuinely furious polar bears** bound to the goblin wagon.

**The fight at the wagon:**
- Plan: [[Zahir]] and [[Sork the Shadow]] sneak around to a small rise beside the stolen dwarf wagon. [[Zahir]] uses *Speak with Animals* to persuade the polar bears to fight for the gang. [[Nasher]] and [[Borc the Orc]] charge the four goblins pulling the dwarf wagon.
- [[Sork the Shadow]] sneaks like a king. [[Zahir]] does not — caught immediately, the four goblins fire arrows at him.
- [[Nasher]] and [[Borc the Orc]] storm in. [[Zahir]] turns toward the goblin wagon and speaks in bear: a **nat 20** on *Speak with Animals* — the polar bears stop following goblin orders and turn on them. *"STOP — FRIENDS — KILL."* Two of the goblins try to poke the bears with their scimitars — it's roughly the last thing they do.
- [[Nasher]] is forced to heal himself mid-combat after taking a solid scimitar hit.
- The goblin leader climbs up to the rise (where Zahir and Sork had been firing from), realizes he's the last one standing, sprints back to the goblin wagon and lights it with a torch. [[Borc the Orc]] teleports straight into the wagon and starts swinging. [[Nasher]] throws a javelin — misses. The leader gets around to the other side and really gets the fire going. [[Borc the Orc]] kills him.
- The wagon is fully ablaze — glass bottles crack in the flames. Loot lost.

**Aftermath:**
- [[Zahir]] tells the polar bears they are free. [[Nasher]] draws his sword and cuts the ropes binding them to the wagon.
- Found in the dead goblin leader's bone: a **whistle** → when blown it means roughly "pull sled, sled pull" (Zahir translates).
- The gang commands the polar bears to help haul the dwarf wagon back. The bears: hungry — they devour the dead goblins. Not a pretty sight.
- Reward from Hruna, Korux, and Storn: **gemstones worth 300 gp** + a **map** to where their fallen companion lies. The gang is welcomed into **Dwarven Valley's trading post** — not the city itself yet, but a foot in the door.

**Toward [[Dvärgadalen]] – recovery:**
- The return trip with the polar bears hauling is quick. The dwarves are not thrilled about their wagon being pulled by bears instead of their axebeak, but grateful.
- At Dwarven Valley Trading Post: the gang sells off goblin equipment and buys **2 healing potions**.
- They sleep one night at the inn.

**Day 25 – Extra night in [[Dvärgadalen]]:**
- Heavy hail outside. [[Sork the Shadow]] and [[Borc the Orc]] still beaten up. The gang stays another night.
- [[Nasher]] quietly asks the innkeeper about **Dzaan** when the others aren't listening. She claims not to know the name — but it is obvious she is lying.
- That night, [[Borc the Orc]] finally shares why he is in Icewind Dale: he **sailed the Sea of Moving Ice 4–5 months ago and was shipwrecked**. He doesn't know how many of his crew survived the wreck. The boat was called **"The Bad Buoy"**. He asks the gang to keep their ears open for any rumors about survivors from his old crew.

**Day 26 – Tracking [[Garret]] and ascending [[Kelvins Cairn]]:**
- The weather clears — fairly good, a little cloudy. [[Sork the Shadow]] and [[Jarthra]] navigate.
- South of Kelvin's Cairn, the gang finds a **camp with an overturned dog sled**. The dogs are thin but alive, still hitched. The gang feeds them.
- [[Zahir]] (reminded to actually cast the spell first) talks to the lead dog **Boy** via *Speak with Animals*: Boy is completely fixated on finding his "Master" — confirming [[Garret]] is up the mountain.
- [[Jarthra]] stays at the abandoned camp. The gang unhitches Boy and follows him up.
- The climb is brutal — sharp wind, treacherous terrain, icy rock face.
- Mountain goats on the way: Zahir speaks to them and they report that **4–5 people** passed them going up earlier.
- An **avalanche** hits the gang in a narrow passage. All athletics checks fail — the gang trips over each other in a panic. The avalanche carries them **~1,000 feet down**. [[Nasher]] digs himself out unaided; pulls up [[Borc the Orc]], then [[Sork the Shadow]] and [[Borc the Orc]] pull out [[Zahir]]. [[Sork the Shadow]] is seriously hurt — [[Nasher]] heals him with the last of his healing magic.
- The gang resumes the brutal climb past the avalanche site.
- They find **[[Garret]]** in the snow — alive but badly injured, bloodied, in shock. He can barely speak. [[Nasher]] heals him with the very last of his power.
- Before they can get a single word out of Garret, **2 large white cats** (crag cats) attack from behind — a surprise. The cats used Garret as bait. [[Nasher]] sets one ablaze with Tyr's holy vengeance. The gang kills both cats together — remarkably lucky, for once.
- Garret tells them: he and [[The Burning Hammers]] were attacked by a **yeti** higher up the mountain. He barely escaped. The other Hammers — unknown status.
- [[Boy]] is reunited with his owner.

**Session end:** Right after the crag cat fight — [[Garret]] rescued, the gang battered, still on the slopes of Kelvin's Cairn. The yeti's fate unresolved. [[The Burning Hammers]] fate unknown.
