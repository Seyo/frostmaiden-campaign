# Session 1 – The Road to Caer Konig

**Day 1 – Fireshear:**
- [[Nasher]] arrives in Fireshear. Meets [[Zahir]] (halfling warlock) and [[Sork the Shadow]] (orc) at a tavern. [[Borc the Orc]] (goliath) offered lodging and joined the group heading north.

**Days 2–5 – Learning the Ropes in Fireshear:**
- [[Aubril(†)]] (merchant, Torg's Imports) hired the party for 75 gp to escort him and his cargo to [[Bryn Shander]].
- Rumours: Invisible thieves in [[Caer Konig]], contact [[Trovus]] might need help there.
- Rumours: A murderer in Ten-Towns — at least 22 victims total, at least 5 confirmed. Ice pick through the heart.
- Learned about [[Auril]] and her curse over Icewind Dale.
- [[Zahir]] is from Calimport in the south, fleeing something. Morally questionable.
- [[Borc the Orc]] raised among giants on Moonshine Isle.

**Day 6:**
- Left Fireshear. At the border of Icewind Dale a magical darkness fell — Nasher's *Light* spell did not function.
- Extreme wind and thick fog forced the group to halt.
- Wolves attacked. The pack leader an enormous wolf. [[Nasher]] fell in battle but survived.

**Day 7:**
- The group woke at camp — the wolves had killed [[Aubril(†)]] and left the body in pieces. Zahir proposed blowing up the body with magic; [[Nasher]] refused.
- [[Sork the Shadow]] misread the trail and headed back toward Fireshear. The group was forced to double back and camp at the exact same spot again.

**Day 8:**
- Arrived in [[Bryn Shander]] late in the evening.

**Day 9:**
- Visited [[Torg]] (*Torg's Imports and Goods*). [[Zahir]] negotiated half the fee despite Aubril's death. [[Torg]] asked the party to seek out [[Hildur Trollbane]].
- [[Nasher]] investigated rumours, learned of *Reindil's Emporium* (half-elf, high-end gear) and *House of the Triad* (temple).

**Days 10–15 – Rest in Bryn Shander:**
- Lodged at *The Hooked Knucklehead*. [[Nasher]] established himself as a helpful adventurer.
- New murder rumours: halfling hunter in Easthaven, human shipbuilder in Targos, dwarf in [[Caer Konig]] — all with ice pick through the heart.
- Met [[Hildur Trollbane]] (scarred dwarf, friend of [[Torg]]) at a tavern. Asked the party to investigate the murders, suspects a connection to [[Auril]] or a cult. Sent them to [[Caer Konig]].
- [[Hildur Trollbane]] noted that the party has a bad reputation in town (Sork's blundering).

**Day 16 – Journey to Caer Konig:**
- Rented a dog sled with 6 dogs.
- Violent hailstorm — 2 dogs died. Stopped at [[Caer Dineval]], paid fines, bought shovels.
- Arrived in [[Caer Konig]] late in the evening.
