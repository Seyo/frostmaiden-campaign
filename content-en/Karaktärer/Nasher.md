Elf paladin, Order of Tyr (Avenger — show no mercy to the wicked). From Neverwinter. High charisma and a strong moral compass. Speaks Dwarvish. Carries a grappling hook and javelins.

- Fell in battle during the wolf attack (Day 6) and again against [[Nildar Sunblight(†)]] (Day 18).
- Killed [[Eevie(†)]] in the fortress cell block in self-defense (fungus-infected) — carries the guilt. See [[Nashers misstag]]. Wrapped her in his own blanket and carried her out with reverence — shed a tear, the first time since Letharia was executed.
- Refuses to let [[Zahir]] desecrate corpses or take shortcuts at the expense of others' dignity. Grudgingly concedes his value when the charm delivers — "He's actually pretty useful sometimes."
- Found a **business ledger** in the Sunblight fortress on Day 19 proving that [[Glenn]] (*The Hook, Line and Sinker*) has been buying ale from the Sunblight dwarves for roughly 2 years. Confronted [[Jarthra]] directly — she is not the buyer (stopped delivering to Glenn about 2 years ago, matching the ledger). No confrontation with Glenn yet.
- Forgot the goats in the fortress cages. Realized it all the way back in Caer Konig: *"DAMN IT! WE FORGOT THE GOATS!"* (Day 19).
- Was deeply disturbed by [[Zahir]]'s and [[Jarthra]]'s discussion about life being better when people were sacrificed to [[Auril]], during the journey back to Caer Konig.
- Spent the rest period (Days 19–23) preaching about Tyr to the townsfolk, and the evenings getting to know the others at the tavern — now knows more about Zahir's and Sork's pasts.
- Is aware of **Dzaan** — asked the innkeeper at [[Dvärgadalen]] about him (Day 25); she claimed not to know the name, but was obviously lying.
- Walked straight up to the unknown dwarves (Hruna, Korux, and Storn) in the cave north of [[Dvärgadalen]] (Day 24) and introduced himself — turned out to be friendly, not Duergar.
- Healed [[Sork the Shadow]] after the avalanche on Day 26 — much appreciated. Also healed [[Garret]] after the gang found him badly wounded in the snow.

## Background

Trained at **The Hall of Justice** in Neverwinter to become a Justiciar of Tyr — Tyr's arm on earth, sent to uphold justice and protect the innocent. Dispatched by the temple northward to [[Ten-Towns]] to aid its inhabitants in their struggle against [[Auril]]'s curse and the dangers plaguing the region.

This is his mission — not a choice made by chance but a calling he carries with him every day.
